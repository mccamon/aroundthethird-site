Welcome to AroundTheThird.org

Mike and Jody McCamon founded AroundTheThird to gift and bring visibility to causes and nonprofits serving or located in the Kansas 3rd Congressional District. Certainly the most lasting impact on us from the 2018 Democratic Primary for us were the many dedicated and wonderful people who serve our neighbors in need.
